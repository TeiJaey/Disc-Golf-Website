Disc Golf Rules

Stick to these simple ground rules so your round stays smooth, fair, and fun for everyone.

  1. Tee Off: You begin each hole from a designated tee area (usually a concrete or turf pad). You must throw the disc from behind this designated line.
  2. Play From the Lie: Wherever your disc lands becomes the spot for your next throw, which is called your "lie". You must place one foot directly behind where your disc came to rest before making your next throw.
  3. Reach the Target: You continue throwing from where the disc lands until it lands inside the metal chains or the basket itself.
  4. Scoring: Each throw counts as one stroke. Just like ball golf, holes are assigned a "Par" (the expected number of throws). The lowest score wins.
